Focused Management, Inc.(FMI) is an Information Technology Services firm that employs cutting-edge technologies and industry best practices to design, develop, and implement innovative solutions to address the complex and dynamic IT challenges of our federal and commercial clients. As a ‘quality-centric’ company, our focus is always on exceptional performance, continuous process improvement, and high-quality service delivery. FMI is a CMMI Level 3 appraised company with the following ISO certifications: ISO 9001:2015, ISO 20000-1:2011, and ISO 27001:2013.
