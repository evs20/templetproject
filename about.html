ABOUT

TELOD, Inc.(FMI) is an Information Technology Services firm that employs cutting-edge technologies and industry best practices to design, develop, and implement innovative solutions to address the complex and dynamic IT challenges of our federal and commercial clients. As a ‘quality-centric’ company, our focus is always on exceptional performance, continuous process improvement, and high-quality service delivery. FMI is a CMMI Level 3 appraised company with the following ISO certifications: ISO 9001:2015, ISO 20000-1:2011, and ISO 27001:2013.

At FMI, we focus on enterprise integration to ensure an optimal solution to meeting your information technology needs. For over two decades, our company has provided professional services to clients in both government and commercial markets by relying on the vast expertise of our many resources and professionals to get the job done right.

As a prime contractor, FMI has successfully managed a wide range of government contracts, including over 30 large-scale contracts, while delivering an extensive variety of services to our customers. Areas of support include Information Technology (IT), Network Management, Systems Analysis and Design, Database Development and Management, Programming, Information Assurance (IA), Program Management, Training, Documentation, Help Desk, Product Management, Consulting and Administrative services.

Founded in 1994, FMI is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) with headquarters in Alexandria, Virginia and a regional office in Kansas City, Missouri. We provide support services in all 50 States and U.S. Territories. FMI successfully graduated from the US Small Business Administration 8(a) program in 2009.
